Who We Are

Rooted in Kashmir.
Present in Dubai.

For generations, our family has walked the villages of Kashmir — sitting with weavers, learning their language, understanding the patience that goes into every single thread.

What began as a deep love for our craft became a mission: to bring the most authentic Kashmiri shawls to the world, without losing a single thread of their story.

The Beginning

Born from the Mountains

It started in the valleys of Kashmir, where the air is crisp and time moves slowly. Growing up surrounded by weavers, our founder learned that true craftsmanship cannot be rushed — it must be felt.

Every shawl tells the story of its maker — the hours spent at the loom, the choices of colour, the precision of every stitch. These are not products. They are living pieces of Kashmiri heritage.

Dubai Chapter

Kashmir Meets the World

The decision to open in Dubai was driven by one belief — that the world deserved access to the finest Kashmiri shawls, presented with the care and dignity they deserve.

Today, from our boutique in Dubai, we welcome collectors, gift-givers, and lovers of fine craft from across the globe. Each visitor leaves not just with a shawl, but with a piece of Kashmir.

Our Values

✦
Authenticity

Every piece we sell is genuinely handmade in Kashmir. We visit the craftsmen ourselves, ensuring no shortcuts and no compromises on quality.

◈
Craftsmanship

We believe in the value of human hands. Machine-made imitations flood the market — we exist to offer something different, something irreplaceable.

❋
Fair Trade

We pay our artisans fairly and work with them directly. When you buy from us, you support a family in Kashmir, not a factory.
