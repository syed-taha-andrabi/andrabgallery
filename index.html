Our Story

Rooted in Kashmir

For generations, our family has walked the villages of Kashmir — sitting with weavers, learning their language, understanding the patience that goes into every thread.

What began as a deep love for our craft became a mission: to bring the most authentic Kashmiri shawls to the world, without losing a single thread of their story.

Today, from our boutique in Dubai, we connect artisans with collectors who understand that true luxury isn't about price — it's about irreplaceable human craft.

Our Specialties

The Art of Kashmir

Each weave tells a different story. Each stitch carries centuries of tradition.

Pashmina
Cashmere Goat Wool

The finest natural fibre in the world, hand-spun and woven into shawls so soft they feel like a second skin.

Kani
Loom Woven

Intricate patterns woven directly into the fabric using wooden sticks. Each shawl can take months to complete.

Sozni
Needle Embroidery

Floral motifs embroidered by hand, stitch by stitch. No two pieces are ever exactly the same.

Jamawar
Jacquard Weave

Rich, heavy shawls with complex brocade patterns — traditionally worn by royalty across the subcontinent.
