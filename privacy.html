Privacy Policy

Last updated — June 2026

Who we are

Andrab Gallery is the online presence of Kashmir Shawl Co., a Dubai-based boutique offering authentic handmade Kashmiri shawls. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit andrabgallery.com and how we use it.

What we collect

This website does not require accounts, does not process payments, and does not store customer data on its own servers. The information involved is limited to the following.

Browsing analytics. We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site — pages viewed, time spent, approximate location, and device type. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you. Google Analytics uses cookies; you can block these in your browser settings or with an ad blocker without affecting your use of the site.

Enquiries you send. When you contact us through WhatsApp, email, or the enquiry form, you share information with us directly — such as your name, contact details, and what you're looking for. We use it only to respond to your enquiry and arrange your purchase. We do not sell or share it with third parties.

Third-party services

The site links to WhatsApp and Instagram, and uses Google Fonts and Google Analytics. When you use those services, their own privacy policies apply. We encourage you to review them.

Your choices

You can browse this site without providing any personal information. If you have contacted us and would like your information removed from our records, write to us at hello@andrabgallery.com and we will delete it.

Questions

For anything related to this policy, contact us at hello@andrabgallery.com or through our contact page.
